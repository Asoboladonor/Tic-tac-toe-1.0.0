How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
2. You are X, and the computer (or your opponent) is O.
3. Players take turns placing their marks (X or O) in an empty square.
4. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) wins the game.
5. If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
